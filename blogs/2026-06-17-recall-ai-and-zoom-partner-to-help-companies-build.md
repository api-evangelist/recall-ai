Recall.ai has partnered with Zoom as a Zoom Preferred Partner, integrating with Zoom's Realtime Media Streams (RTMS) API to give developers access to audio, video, transcripts, and metadata. The platform supports over 3,000 companies building meeting assistants, AI sales coaches, and notetakers without managing complex WebSocket infrastructure, with pricing starting at $0.50 per hour of meeting recording.

This guide walks through every step of submitting a Zoom app for marketplace approval, covering eight steps from creating the app and obtaining development credentials to documenting OAuth scopes, completing the listing, and submitting a test plan with a video walkthrough. Reviews typically take 5-7 business days, with incomplete scope descriptions and missing documentation being common rejection reasons.

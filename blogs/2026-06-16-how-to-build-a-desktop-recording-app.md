This tutorial shows how to build a bot-free desktop recording app similar to Granola using Recall.ai's Desktop Recording SDK. It covers detecting and recording meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Slack Huddles, plus transcript retrieval with speaker identification and AI summarization, using a stack of Electron, Express, and OpenAI APIs.

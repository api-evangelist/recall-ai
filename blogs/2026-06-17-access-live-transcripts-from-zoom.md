This article outlines four approaches for obtaining live Zoom transcripts: a meeting participant bot, on-device capture sent to a speech-to-text provider, Zoom RTMS for live media access, and RTMP live streaming to a developer-controlled endpoint. It recommends a cross-platform approach like Recall.ai that works across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and more for end-user products.

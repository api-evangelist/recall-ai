The Google Meet Media API provides access to live RTP audio and video streams (including screensharing) during Google Meet calls via WebRTC, rather than post-call artifacts. It requires restricted OAuth scopes with a 4-7 week security assessment, all participants in Google's developer preview, host consent, a minimum 4 Mbps bandwidth, and developer-implemented codec, SDP, and ICE handling, making it best suited for internal tooling.

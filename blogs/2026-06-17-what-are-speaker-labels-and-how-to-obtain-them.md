Speaker labels, also called speaker diarization, identify who spoke each word in a transcript, giving LLMs context to extract sales pain points, action items, or patient symptoms. The article explains you can obtain labels either through the Recall.ai API, which provides real participant names from video conferencing platforms, or through machine diarization, which assigns generic placeholders like "Speaker 1" or "Speaker 2".

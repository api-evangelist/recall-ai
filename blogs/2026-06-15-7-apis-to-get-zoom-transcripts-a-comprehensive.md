This guide explores eight methods for obtaining Zoom transcripts programmatically, evaluating each on cost, real-time availability, speaker identification accuracy, maintenance burden, and integration complexity. It positions Recall.ai (Method 7) as a managed solution requiring about 72 hours to integrate, versus custom meeting bots (Method 6) that can demand over a year of engineering across three platforms.
